A valve that seals itself. The disc and seat design utilizes fluid pressure to ensure a secure seal. In other words, the higher the pressure, the better the sealing performance. Valve can be installed in either direction.

Touch seat design. Thanks to its double eccentric design, valve disc is only in contact with the seat ring when completely closed. This ensures longer service life, low seating torque and leak-tight shutoff.

Metal or Teflon. Metal seat ring can handle temperatures up to 450 ℃ while Teflon can handle temperatures up to 230 ℃.
